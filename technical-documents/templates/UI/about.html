Our Ethos

At our sustainability app for the University of Exeter, our ethos is centered around creating a better future for both the university and the planet. We believe that every small action can make a big impact, and it is up to us as a community to work together towards a sustainable future.


We strive to promote awareness and education about sustainable practices, empowering individuals to make informed decisions that positively impact the environment. Our app provides easy-to-use tools and resources to help users reduce their carbon footprint, conserve energy and water, and minimize waste.


We are committed to creating a culture of sustainability on campus by partnering with local businesses, organizations, and student groups to promote eco-friendly initiatives and practices. We believe in the power of collaboration and recognize that sustainable change requires a collective effort.


As a sustainability app, we are dedicated to transparency and accountability. We are constantly striving to improve our own sustainability practices and to make our app more user-friendly and accessible. We value feedback from our users and are always open to suggestions for improvement.


Ultimately, our goal is to create a more sustainable future for the University of Exeter and beyond. By working together, we can make a positive impact on the environment and create a better world for future generations.
